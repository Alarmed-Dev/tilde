About

Tilde

The students note taking application of the future.

Tilde is a student-orientated, hyper-organized note taking application for power users, created as a school project.

Version 1.0

What is Tilde?

Tilde was built on the foundational idea of an "eNotebook", or a hardware / software replacement for a traditional notebook used in school environments.

It all started as a school project where students were tasked with creating and marketing a product that would be sold at a "marketplace" the following month, where all of the profits would go to charity. A similar competition was introduced the group earlier that year where the project started, though things didn't play out in the end.

Already having a half complete product, the project was continued as Tilde, leading us to today. Ditch the notebooks and use your mobile device.
